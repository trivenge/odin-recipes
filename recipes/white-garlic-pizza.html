White Garlic Pizza Sauce

A creamy and versatile garlic white sauce that is essential and delicious for about any pizza, pasta, or other recipes!

Sauce at a Glance:

  • Preparation time of 2 minutes
  • Cook time of 10 minutes
  • Total time of 12 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 4 cloves of minced garlic
  • 1/4 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 2-3 tablespoons of flour
  • 1 and 1/2 cups of half & half milk

  1. Step 1:

    • Heat oil in a skillet on medium to high heat.

  2. Step 2:

    • Add the garlic and continue heating for about 3 minutes until garlic is fragrant.

  3. Step 3:

    • Add the flour (consider trying 2 tablespoons first before adding a third), oregano, pepper, and salt. Whisk well.

  4. Step 4:

    • Add the half & half milk while whisking constantly. Cook and stir until bubbly and thickened, which takes about 3 minutes.

  5. Side Note:

    • To thicken the sauce, mix the remaining 1 tablespoon of flour with a some milk until you have a mashed potato consistency. Pour that mixture into the bubbling sauce and whisk constantly. Cook about 1 additional minute. This should thicken the sauce. Repeat it until you have the desired thickness if necessary.
